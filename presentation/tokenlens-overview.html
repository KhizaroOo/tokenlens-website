How it works

Two simple flows

Getting your AI numbers
1You add your AI tool's key in Settings
2We lock it away with strong encryption
3A worker fetches your usage automatically
4Numbers show up on your dashboard
When someone contacts us
1They fill the contact or demo form
2We block spam and bots automatically
3The message is saved safely (no raw IP)
4The sales team gets an email
Privacy first

We only store the numbers

  • Never your prompts, answers, or code — only cost & usage
  • API keys locked with strong encryption
  • Each company only sees its own data
  • No fake claims — we say only what is true
